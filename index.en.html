About us?

We are a network of strategic alliances between local churches and mission organizations working towards a vision.

Mission

Reach the world with the gospel.

Vision

Achieve the vision by establishing strategic links.

Services

- Make it easier for the local church to send missionaries.
- Link churches through strategic alliances based on projects.
- Raise awareness of the responsibility of the local church in relation to the global mission.
- Provide pastoral care to missionaries.
- Promote missionary intercession.
- Disseminate missionary projects in the network of associated churches.
-Enable missionary involvement through the volunteer system.

What do we believe?

Although at MAIN we work together with people and churches with different Christian traditions, we agree on the importance of the authority of the Bible and accept the general parameters of the Lausanne covenant. For this reason, we have adopted the conditions dictated in the Lausanne Pact as the basis for our development.

OUR HISTORY

Main was originally born as a group called “Friends of Equatorial Guinea”. Passionate about the mission and dedicated to work in that place, back in the 80's, a work began that was expanding more and more. Today, MAIN works on every continent, giving pastoral support to missionaries, and collaborating so that the message of Jesus Christ reaches every corner of the planet.
